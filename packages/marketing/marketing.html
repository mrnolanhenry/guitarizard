Guitarizard is your one-stop scale shop.

Tools we offer

Virtual Fretboard (and Keyboard)

Displays musical keys/scales.

Common Tuning Presets

Want to play the phrygian scale on your guitar in DADGAD or Open G tuning?

Guitarizard will show you how.

Custom Tunings

Found a unique tuning you use for your banjo?

Guitarizard can show you how to play in the key of 'D# bebop dorian' in whatever funky tuning you choose.

Scalebook

We offer a longer list of scales than most sites, many outside of typical western music, and are always looking to add more.

Searching for Keys

Quickly find a key by searching for its name, e.g. 'C# mixolydian' or just 'mixolydian'

Don't know the name? Find any key by some of the notes you're playing,
e.g. 'A, B, C#, D, F, Gb.'

Equivalent Keys

Let's say you're playing notes that fit in 'E major,' but you feel like the root is in 'Db.'
If you know your theory, you might know that you're actually playing in 'Db aeolian.'

What about if you're playing notes that seem to fit in 'E dorian #4?'

Guitarizard will let you know you're looking for 'Db locrian 6.'
